About the Game

What Is Stunt Bike Extreme?

Stunt Bike Extreme is a bike stunt game made for players who enjoy racing, jumps, and skill-based tracks. You ride a dirt bike, cross obstacles, and try to finish each level without crashing. The game mixes motorcycle physics with stunt racing — you need speed, balance, and patience.

A small mistake on a ramp or landing can slow your time or stop your run entirely. The game is built around short levels, quick restarts, and bike upgrades. It works well for players who want fast gameplay on mobile, desktop, or browser. Developed by Hyperkani, it has been downloaded over 50 million times worldwide.

Player Guide

How to Play Stunt Bike Extreme

Master the controls and ride your way to the top

🕹️

Basic Controls and Bike Balance

  • Use the gas button to speed up; brake to slow down
  • On mobile, tilt your device or use on-screen lean buttons
  • On PC, use arrow keys or WASD to steer and lean
  • Keeping balance during a jump matters as much as speed
🏁

How to Complete Levels

  • Each level has a start point, obstacles, and a finish line
  • Cross the finish without crashing to pass the level
  • Some levels add time limits or minimum star targets
  • Patience matters more than raw speed on tight tracks
⭐

How to Earn Stars and Rewards

  • Stars come from completing levels cleanly
  • Three stars require a crash-free run with a fast time
  • Coins come from level completion and bonus pickups
  • Use coins to upgrade or unlock new bikes
🔧

How to Upgrade Your Bike

  • Go to the garage and pick any bike you own
  • Use coins or earned rewards to upgrade stats
  • Upgrades improve speed, control, and stability
  • Test each upgrade on a familiar track to feel the change
Pro Advice

Gameplay Tips

Improve your score and ride further with these tips

🌱

Beginner Tips

Start slow, especially on ramp sections
Focus on landing straight before chasing speed
Replay early levels to build control confidence
Finish the level first — chase 3 stars on the second run
🏍️

Best Bike and Upgrade Tips

Upgrade stability before upgrading speed
Use heavier bikes for obstacle tracks, lighter for jumps
Avoid spending all coins on a single upgrade at once
Test each upgrade on a track you already know well
📈

Tips to Improve Your Score

Keep the bike straight just before landing from a jump
Avoid extra flips when the landing zone is short
Replay levels after each upgrade to see the difference
Clean driving — no crashes — gives the biggest score boost
