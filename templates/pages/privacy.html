01

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Stunt Bike Extreme Fan Guide website collects, uses, and protects any information that may be gathered when you visit or use our website. Please read this policy carefully before using our website.

By using this website, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. This policy applies to all pages and content published on this website, including guides, blog posts, download information, and any forms we operate.

02

Who We Are

We are an independent fan guide website focused on providing informational content about Stunt Bike Extreme. We are not the official developer, publisher, or owner of the game or any related application.

ℹ️
Not an Official Platform This website is not connected with Hyperkani, Google Play, Apple App Store, or any official game platform. We are an independent fan and informational website only.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your personal data, you can reach us through our Contact page.

03

Information We May Collect

Depending on how you interact with our website, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Pages visited and content read during your visit
  • Time and duration spent on individual pages
  • Browser type, language, and version
  • Operating system and device type
  • Approximate geographic location (country or region level only)
  • Referring website or source that brought you to our website
  • Search terms used to find our website through search engines
  • Interactions with advertisements displayed on our pages
  • Information submitted through our contact form (name, email, subject, message)
  • Cookie data as described in our Cookie Policy

We do not collect payment information, identification documents, passport details, or any sensitive personal data through this website.

04

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve this website and its content
  • Understand how visitors use our content and which guides are most helpful
  • Respond to messages or questions submitted through our contact form
  • Identify and fix technical issues, broken links, or page loading errors
  • Display relevant advertisements through third-party ad networks
  • Monitor for spam, abuse, or malicious activity on our forms
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations if required by law
  • Analyze overall traffic trends to improve content quality over time
  • Understand visitor interests to provide more relevant game guides

We do not use your information for unsolicited marketing, automated profiling for commercial decisions, or any purpose beyond what is described in this policy.

05

Contact Form Information

When you use our contact form, we collect the name, email address, subject, and message you provide. This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry.

✅
Contact Data Use We do not add contact form submissions to mailing lists, share them with third parties for marketing purposes, or use them for anything other than responding to your specific inquiry.

We retain contact form submissions for a reasonable period to allow for follow-up communication, then delete them in accordance with our data retention practices.

06

Cookies and Tracking

This website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience, remember your preferences, and collect anonymous usage analytics. For full details on the types of cookies we use and how to control them, please read our Cookie Policy.

You can disable or manage cookies at any time through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect how this website functions for you.

07

Analytics

We use third-party analytics services, such as Google Analytics, to understand how visitors interact with this website. These services collect anonymized data about:

  • Pages visited and navigation paths through the website
  • Time spent on the website and on individual pages
  • Traffic sources (search engines, direct visits, referrals)
  • Browser type and device information
  • Approximate geographic location (country or region only)

All analytics data is aggregated and anonymized. We cannot identify individual visitors through analytics reports. Google Analytics is governed by Google's own Privacy Policy.

08

Advertising

This website may display advertisements served by Google AdSense or other third-party advertising networks. These networks may use cookies and web beacons to collect information about your visits to this and other websites in order to display relevant ads.

ℹ️
Ad Personalization Opt-Out You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings or using the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out tool. These options let you control how your browsing data is used for ad targeting.

We have no access to or control over the cookies or tracking technologies used by advertising networks. Their data collection and use is governed by their own privacy policies.

09

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external websites for reference or additional information. These links are not endorsements and we have no control over the privacy practices or content of those websites.

Once you leave our website by clicking an external link, this Privacy Policy no longer applies. We strongly encourage you to review the privacy policy of any external website you visit before sharing any personal information.

10

APK Download Safety

This website provides informational guides about APK downloads. We do not host or distribute APK files directly. Any APK download that occurs happens through third-party platforms, which have their own separate privacy and data practices.

⚠️
Third-Party Download Risk When downloading files from external sources, your data may be processed independently by those platforms. Always review the privacy policy of any platform before downloading files or creating an account.
11

Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has submitted personal information to this website, please contact us immediately and we will take prompt steps to remove that information.

If you are under 13 years of age, please do not use this website or submit any personal information through our contact form.

12

Data Protection

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect any information collected through this website against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

However, no method of data transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

13

Data Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy. Contact form submissions are retained for a reasonable period to allow for follow-up communication, then securely deleted.

Analytics data collected through Google Analytics is retained according to Google's own data retention settings and policies. Cookie data is retained based on each individual cookie's expiry period.

14

Your Choices

You have several options to control how your information is collected and used:

  • Manage or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time
  • Opt out of Google Analytics tracking via the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Opt out of personalized advertising through Google's Ad Settings
  • Avoid submitting personal information through our contact form if preferred
  • Use private or incognito browsing mode to limit cookie storage on your device
  • Contact us to request deletion of any personal data we may hold about you
15

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain privacy rights under applicable law, including:

  • Right to access the personal information we hold about you
  • Right to request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data
  • Right to request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to object to or restrict the processing of your data
  • Right to withdraw consent where processing is based on your consent
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a relevant data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us through our Contact page. We will respond to all valid requests within a reasonable timeframe.

16

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or applicable legal requirements. When we make updates, we will revise the "Last Updated" date shown at the top of this page.

We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about how we protect your privacy. Your continued use of this website after any changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
